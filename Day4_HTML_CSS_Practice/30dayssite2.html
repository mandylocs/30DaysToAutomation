About the Book

Why Read It?

A machine that predicts the future — until it starts looping time itself.

In The Oracles Loop, an algorithm known as the Oracle begins to repeat fragments of the past. When memory collapses and humanity forgets who they are, one woman must confront the system she helped build. Fast-paced, emotional, and thought-provoking, this book explores memory, technology, and destiny.
